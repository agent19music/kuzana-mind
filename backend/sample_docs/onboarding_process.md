# Employee Onboarding Process

## Overview

All new employees must complete the onboarding process within their first two weeks. HR coordinates the process and will send a welcome pack on day one.

## Before Day One

- IT will provision your laptop and send it to your address three days before your start date.
- You will receive a welcome email from HR with your temporary credentials for Google Workspace.
- Complete the background check form sent via email.

## Week One Checklist

- Attend the company orientation session on Monday morning.
- Set up your Google Workspace account and enable 2FA.
- Meet your assigned buddy from the People team.
- Complete mandatory compliance training modules in the learning portal.
- Submit your signed employment contract to HR via DocuSign.

## Week Two Checklist

- Complete role-specific tool access requests via the IT portal.
- Set up your billing profile in the finance system if your role requires it.
- Schedule a 1:1 with your direct manager to align on 30/60/90 day goals.

## Contacts

For any onboarding questions, contact the HR team at hr@athena.co.
