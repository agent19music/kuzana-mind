# Expense Claims

## Overview

Employees may claim reimbursement for reasonable business expenses. All claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred.

## Eligible Expenses

- Travel: flights, trains, taxis, and car hire for client or company business.
- Accommodation: hotels for overnight business travel, up to $200 per night without prior approval.
- Meals: up to $50 per day when travelling for business. Team meals require manager approval.
- Equipment: peripherals and office supplies up to $100 without approval. Anything above requires Finance sign-off.
- Software: must be on the approved software list or have prior IT approval.

## How to Submit a Claim

1. Collect all receipts. Digital receipts are accepted.
2. Log in to the finance system at finance.athena.co.
3. Navigate to Expense Claims and click New Claim.
4. Enter each line item, attach the receipt, and assign it to the correct project code.
5. Submit the claim. Your manager will approve it within 3 business days.
6. Approved claims are paid on the next payroll run.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment budget).
- Personal subscriptions or software not approved by IT.
- Fines or penalties of any kind.
- Expenses without a receipt.

## International Travel

For international travel, get manager approval before booking. Use the company travel agent for flights where possible. Claim foreign currency expenses at the exchange rate on the date of the transaction.
