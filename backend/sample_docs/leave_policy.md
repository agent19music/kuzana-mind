# Leave Policy

## Overview

Kuzana operates a flexible leave policy. All leave must be approved by your direct manager and logged in the HR system at least 48 hours in advance except in emergencies.

## Annual Leave

- Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year.
- Leave accrues monthly at 1.67 days per month.
- Up to 5 unused days may be carried over to the following year. Remaining days are forfeited.

## Sick Leave

- Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per year.
- For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate is required.
- Sick leave does not carry over and is not paid out on termination.

## Public Holidays

- Employees observe all public holidays in their country of employment.
- Working on a public holiday requires manager approval and is compensated with a day off in lieu.

## How to Request Leave

1. Log in to the HR portal at hr.kuzana.co.
2. Navigate to Leave Requests and select the leave type.
3. Select your dates and add a note for your manager.
4. Your manager will approve or decline within 24 hours.

## Emergency Leave

For emergency leave, notify your manager by phone or Slack immediately. Log the request in the HR portal as soon as possible after.
