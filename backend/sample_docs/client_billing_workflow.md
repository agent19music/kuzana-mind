# Client Billing Workflow

## Overview

All client invoices are generated and tracked through the finance system. The billing cycle runs monthly and invoices must be issued by the 3rd of each month for the prior month's work.

## Invoice Generation

1. Log in to the finance system at finance.athena.co.
2. Navigate to Clients and select the relevant account.
3. Click Generate Invoice and review the line items pulled from the project tracker.
4. Add any manual adjustments with a note explaining the reason.
5. Submit for finance review. David Kariuki approves all invoices above $5,000.

## Payment Terms

- Standard payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date.
- Enterprise clients on annual contracts may have net 60 terms — check the contract.
- Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge after the grace period.

## Disputed Invoices

If a client disputes an invoice, do not issue a credit note without approval from both the Client Success team and Finance. Log the dispute in the finance system under the client account.

## Expense Reimbursements

Client-billable expenses must be approved by your manager before being added to an invoice. Attach receipts in the finance system against the relevant project code.
