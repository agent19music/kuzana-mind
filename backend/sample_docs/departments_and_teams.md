## People & HR

Lead: Amara Osei (Chief People Officer)

### Leave Policy Summary
- Annual leave: 20 days per calendar year
- Sick leave: 10 days (no carry-over)
- Submit requests via the HR portal or email amara.osei@kuzana.co with at least 2 weeks notice for planned leave

### Onboarding
New starters complete onboarding over two weeks:
- Week 1: IT setup, meet the team, read company docs
- Week 2: Shadow each department, complete compliance training

### Performance Reviews
Conducted twice yearly: June and December. Managed by Amara in coordination with each team lead.

## Finance

Lead: David Kariuki (CFO)

### Expense Claims
- Submit by the 25th of each month for same-month processing
- Attach receipts for anything over $20
- Reimbursement paid on the last working day of the month

### Invoicing
- Client invoices raised by David on the 1st of each month
- Net 30 payment terms standard
- Escalate overdue invoices (>45 days) to David immediately

### Procurement
All software or hardware purchases over $200 require David's sign-off before ordering.
