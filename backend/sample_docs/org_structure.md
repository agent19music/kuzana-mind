# Athena Organisation Structure

## Overview

Athena is a 11-person team across six departments. All departments report directly to the CEO except Client Success, which sits under Sales, and IT, which sits under Engineering.

---

## Executive

**Chidi Okeke** — Chief Executive Officer  
Responsible for company strategy, investor relations, board reporting, and final sign-off on all major business decisions. The exec team meets every Monday at 9:00 AM.

---

## Engineering

**Siyanda Nkosi** — Chief Technology Officer  
Owns the technical roadmap, architecture decisions, and engineering hiring.

Direct reports:
- **Naledi Dlamini** — IT Manager: laptop provisioning, tool access, VPN, software licenses
- **Tariq Osman** — Senior Software Engineer: backend systems, APIs, database
- **Amina Diallo** — Product Designer: UX, Figma, prototypes, branding

Engineering standups: Mon/Wed/Fri at 10:00 AM.  
Code reviews are mandatory before any merge to main.

---

## People & HR

**Amara Osei** — Chief People Officer  
Owns recruitment, onboarding, offboarding, leave management, performance reviews, and company culture initiatives.

HR queries (leave requests, contracts, payroll): email amara.osei@athena.co or use the #hr-requests Slack channel.

---

## Finance

**David Kariuki** — Chief Financial Officer  
Owns budgeting, expense reimbursements, client invoicing, payroll processing, and annual audits.

Expense claims must be submitted by the 25th of each month. Approvals take 3–5 business days.

---

## Sales & Client Success

**Fatima Al-Hassan** — VP of Sales  
Owns the revenue pipeline, pricing decisions, partnership agreements, and sales tooling.

Direct reports:
- **Kofi Mensah** — Head of Client Success: account management, SLA tracking, client escalations

New client onboarding is coordinated between Sales and Client Success within the first 48 hours of contract sign.

---

## Legal

**Zanele Mokoena** — General Counsel  
Reviews all contracts, NDAs, GDPR compliance, and IP matters. Legal review typically takes 2–3 business days. Do not sign any external agreement without Zanele's sign-off.

---

## Marketing

**Blessing Okafor** — Marketing Manager  
Owns content strategy, social media, newsletters, events, and all external communications. All press enquiries must be routed through Blessing before any response is sent.
