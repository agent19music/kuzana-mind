## Objective

Convert hackathon demo into 5–10 demo bookings and 2–3 pilot commitments within 2 weeks of demo day.

---

## Audience

**Primary:** Operations managers and HR leads at 15–80 person companies in Lagos, Nairobi, Accra, Johannesburg. Google Workspace users. Currently drowning in internal questions.

**Secondary:** Founders at seed-to-Series-A startups (10–40 people) who are the de facto ops lead and want out of the loop.

**Tertiary:** Technical judges, accelerator partners, and ecosystem connectors who can amplify.

---

## Core Message

Your team's knowledge is already documented. Kuzana Brain makes it findable — instantly, from a chat interface, with zero hallucination risk.

---

## Proof Point

Live demo: 7 company docs ingested in under 60 seconds. Natural language queries return verbatim answers with source citations. Undocumented queries route to the right staff member by name, title, and email.

## Demo Day Execution

1. **Open with the problem** (30 sec) — use the Setup section from `demo-script.md`
2. **Live product walkthrough** (4 min) — Scenes 1–5 in order
3. **Close with the roadmap** (1 min) — Drive sync, Slack integration, usage analytics
4. **Collect contacts** — anyone who engages gets a follow-up email within 24 hours

| Risk | Mitigation |

| Live demo breaks | Fallback screen recording ready. Test 30 min before. |
| Low event attendance | LinkedIn posts reach beyond the room. Cold outreach runs regardless. |
| No demo bookings | Shift to personal DMs and WhatsApp. Direct outreach beats broadcast. |
| Questions about pricing | "Early access, no cost for pilot. We're looking for design partners." |
| Questions about data security | "Your docs, your database, your credentials. Nothing shared." |
