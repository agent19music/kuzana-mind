**Kuzana Brain answers your team's questions instantly — from your own docs, or the right person on your team.**

## Positioning Statement (Full)

**For** operations leads, HR managers, and founders at 10–200 person companies in Africa,
**who** waste hours every week tracking down answers that should already be documented somewhere,
**Kuzana Brain** is an AI knowledge assistant
**that** answers team questions instantly from your company docs, and routes to the right person when it doesn't know,
**unlike** generic AI tools that hallucinate or require you to build a new knowledge base from scratch,
**because** Kuzana connects to your existing Google Docs, never fabricates an answer, and always tells you who to ask if the docs come up short.

---

## The Core Tension

> "The information exists. It's in a doc, or in someone's head. But by the time a new hire or a busy manager tracks it down, they've lost 30 minutes and interrupted two people."

Kuzana doesn't create a new source of truth. It makes the one you already have **findable**.

---

## What We Are Not

- Not a wiki builder (you already have Google Docs — we use those)
- Not a chatbot that makes things up (we cite the source or route to a person)
- Not an enterprise intranet (no implementation project, no IT ticket, no 6-month rollout)
- Not a generic AI assistant (it knows *your* company, not the internet)

---

## Brand Voice in One Sentence

Direct. Warm. Specific. Never says "leverage" or "empower". Sounds like a smart colleague, not a sales deck.
