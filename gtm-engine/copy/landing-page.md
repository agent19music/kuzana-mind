## How It Works

### 1. Connect your docs
Point Kuzana at your Google Drive folder. Ingestion takes under 60 seconds.

### 2. Ask a question
Your team types a question in plain English. "What's the leave policy?" "Who handles procurement?" "How do I submit expenses?"

### 3. Get the answer — or the right person
If the answer is in your docs, Kuzana returns the exact text with a source citation. If it's not documented, Kuzana surfaces the right person to ask — name, title, email.

**No hallucinations. No guessing. Every answer is traceable.**

## Features

### Answers from your own documents
Not the internet. Not a training dataset. Your company's actual policies, processes, and org structure — cited with the source document.

### Staff directory routing
When docs don't cover it, Kuzana doesn't make something up. It tells you who on your team owns that topic — with their name, title, and email.

### Zero-hallucination architecture
There's no generative step that could invent information. The answer is either a verbatim excerpt from your document, or a real person's contact details. Nothing in between.

### Works on what you already have
Google Docs. That's it. No migration, no new writing tool, no 6-month implementation. If your docs exist, Kuzana can read them.

### Fast to set up, fast to answer
Ingestion: under 60 seconds. Query response: under 2 seconds. Your team is productive on day one.
